The Examination of
Ada Hamilton
of the Parish of Walthamstow in the County of Essex, wife of jeweller
William Cheeseman
of the Parish of Leyton in the County of Essex, Police Constable
George Folkard
of the Parish of Leyton in the County of Essex, Inspector of Police
James Honey
of the Parish of Leyton in the County of Essex, Police Constable
Henry Wheatley
of the Parish of Leyton in the County of Essex, Police Constable
Henry Gould
of the Parish of Leyton in the County of Essex, Surgeon
taken on Oath this eighteenth day of August in the year of our Lord One Thousand Eight Hundred and Eighty Three
at the Court House, Great Eastern Road, Stratford, in the County aforesaid, before the undersigned, two of Her Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the said County, in the presence and hearing of William Gouldstone
who was charged this day before us for that he the said William Gouldstone
on the eighth day of August One Thousand Eight Hundred and Eighty Three
at the Parish of Walthamstow in the County of Essex, feloniously, wilfully and of his malice aforethought did kill and murder one Charles Gouldstone
and for that he the said William Gouldstone on the eighth day of August 1883 at the said parish of Walthamstow in the said County feloniously, wilfully and of his malice aforethought did kill and murder one Herbert Gouldstone
and for that he the said William Gouldstone on the eighth day of August 1883 at the said parish of Walthamstow in the said County feloniously, wilfully and of his malice aforethought did kill and murder one Frederick Gouldstone
and for that he the said William Gouldstone on the eighth day of August 1883 at the said parish of Walthamstow in the said County feloniously, wilfully and of his malice aforethought did kill and murder two infant male children, the children of the said William Gouldstone
against the Peace of our Sovereign Lady the Queen, Her Crown and Dignity, and contrary to the Statute in such case made and provided.
