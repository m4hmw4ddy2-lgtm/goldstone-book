The Under Secretary of State presents his compliments to the Clerk of the Central Criminal Court and requests that he will have the goodness to acknowledge the receipt of the Depositions in the case of William Gouldstone who was convicted of Murder at the Central Criminal Court in September 1883, which are returned to him herewith.
These Depositions were found in some old Home Office papers and should have been returned many years ago.
A31638
Home Office Whitehall
2nd Dec 1901
