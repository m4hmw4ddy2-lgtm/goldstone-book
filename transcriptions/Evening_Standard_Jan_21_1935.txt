TRANSCRIPTION

Death After 52 Years
in Broadmoor

William Goldstone, a Walthamstow
blacksmith, who 52 years ago was found
guilty of the murder of his five children
and found to be insane, has died in Broad-
moor Criminal Lunatic Asylum. He was
80 years of age, and had been described as
an ideal patient. He worked in the various
wards.
