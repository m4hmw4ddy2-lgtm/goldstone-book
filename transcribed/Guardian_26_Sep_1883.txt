[THE GUARDIAN / MANCHESTER GUARDIAN]
26 September 1883

The execution of Gouldstone, who murdered his five children at Walthamstow, has been respited until the 8th October. An inquiry into the mental condition of the unhappy criminal is to be held. The respite is probably tantamount to a remission of the capital sentence.
